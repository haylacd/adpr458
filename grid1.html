About Picnic

Study

Story

Learning a language can be as easy as a walk in the park.

What is Picnic?

Picnic is an interactive, language learning app aimed to bring you real situations, conversations and language experience.

How It Works

Everyday, users will take part in mini games, bite-sized lessons and easy to follow stories that allow them to practice their target language and learn real-life vocabulary.

Community & Engagement

While users are on their own language journey, they also have the opportunity to engage with the language learning community through polls, posts and discussion boards.
